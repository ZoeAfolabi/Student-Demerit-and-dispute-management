Welcome to The Student Demerit and Dispute Management Sytem

Welcome to our student demerit and dispute management system! This system has been designed to help with keeping track of student demerits and manage any disputes that may arise. With our user-friendly interface administrators can easily log demerits and assign consequences, while students can access their accounts to view their records and dispute any incidents they feel were unfairly assigned. Our system streamlines the process of managing student behavior, creating a fair and transparent environment for all parties involved.
